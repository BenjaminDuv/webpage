Services

London ADC is a company proposing architectural services. Our architects are used to be involved in building projects from the earliest stages right through to completion. We have a great experience within the construction industry, the design of new buildings, the restoration and the conservation of old ones, as well as the development of new ways of using existing ones.


London ADC offers the possibility of achieving several projects :


  • M aster Planning : This document establishes the framework of a whole site, summarizing civil goals and aspirations for the project to give form and organization and to find a realistic plan for implementation.
  • Landscape Design : It is the design of outdoors, in order to achieve environmental, social and esthetical outcomes.
  • Healthcare Building : We have experience in the drawing of hospital plans, between medium to big size. For instance, we are currently planning a hospital in Pakistan, as part of our involvement with the Awaiting Eyes Foundation.
  • Extension / Refurbishment : The services proposed are, for example, the extension of the roof or a part of the house, the building of a porch or outbuildings.
