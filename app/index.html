Who are we ?

If you are looking for an architectural company which realizes your project of creating design building or interior, London Architecture and Design Centre seems perfect for you ! Our company is specialized in drawing the design of buildings and providing architectural services that will improve your everyday comfort. Our team of professionals is very implicated in its work and motivated to provide services of quality thanks to new techniques and technologies. Our priority is to satisfy your social, environmental and economic needs, so there is no reason not to trust us !

What we stand for ?

What makes us different from the other companies is the fact that we donate half of our profits to the Awaiting Eyes Foundation(AEF). AEF provides an access to education, accommodations, clothing and other assistances to orphaned children and widowed mothers in Pakistan. For instance, the AEF volunteers have been working on several projects such as the construction of a hospital, the implementation of a water pump and the plantation of Cassava, an easy to grow and nutritious plant, in the Pakistani region ravaged for the 2005 earthquake.
